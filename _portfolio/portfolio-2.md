A student self-learning platform for the Department of Electronic Engineering at Tsinghua University.

**Project Background:** Challenges faced by university education in the era of AI — the loss of curriculum coherence, making it difficult for students to establish intrinsic logic between courses; the shift in the meaning of learning, where students lose their sense of agency as AI can accomplish most tasks.

**Technical Architecture:** RAG + AI architecture, leveraging GLM models, vector databases, hybrid retrieval, and other technologies to build a knowledge retrieval and learning assistance system, while integrating personalized learning footprints and PBL projects.
