Identifies and systematically analyzes the dual-level (head and token) workload imbalance that arises from applying block-wise sparse attention with existing sequence parallelism methods. Introduces a dual-balanced workload partitioning approach and a sparsity-aware strategy selection mechanism for dynamic selection of the optimal parallel strategy.
